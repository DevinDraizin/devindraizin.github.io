I am an undergraduate student at the University of Central Florida majoring in Computer Engineering with a minor in mathematics. My passion lies in exploring new technologies in electronics, mechanics, software development and more to help solve problems and make people's lives easier. I participate in several computer and technology-focused organizations such as TechKnights (a student organization which centers on building and learning anything tech) and HackUCF (a collegiate cyber defense club which competes in areas such as information security, networking, and software development). Additionally, I have had strong interests in aerospace and rocketry for many years and am a member of the IREC (Intercollegiate Rocket Engineering Competition) team under SEDS (Students for the Exploration and Development of Space) at UCF.
